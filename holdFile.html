Rock Retaining Walls

"25,000 sq ft per year"

We install over 25,000 sqare feet of rock retaining walls per year. We have our own trucks to haul the rock and work with several different quarrys for a variety of stone options. We work with several geotech engineers who can help us design your walls to meet certain codes or structural strengths. Our machines are equipped with hydraulic thumbs to stack rocks fast and tight! Several of our operators are experts in this area and can stack walls with different pattern options or decorative "rockscaping" including beautiful steps, waterfalls, etc. We can bid your entire rock project, work hourly plus materials, or by the square foot. Just contact us for pricing.
