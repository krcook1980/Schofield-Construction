Excavating

We "dig" it all: custom homesites, road construction, commercial site work, complex infastructures, pump stations, and more. With 9 machines, 3 dump trucks, and gravel slinger, we have what your job needs.

Bidding

Using the digital software 'Paydirt' by Trimble we are able to quickly and effectively quantify every detail of your plans. This allows us to provide a complete and accurate bid for your budget. The 3D model helps you visualize your final grade.

Rock Walls

We install over 25,000 ft of rock retaining walls every year. With several quarrys providing a variety of stone options, we can build a structural wall that looks like a piece of art. Include steps, waterfalls, and more to complete your "rockscaping".

Hammering

With two 5,000 lb hammer/breaker attachments for our machines, our machines can easily cut through the toughest rock. Handling rock hammering in house allows us to minimize costs and utilize time wisely for your project.

Hauling

Our 3 dump trucks will haul anything and everything that will fit inside. From clearing and grubbing, importing or exporting materials, to hauling rock, our fleet can handle all of your needs.

Slinging

When it is a tight fit, or a long throw, bring in the slinger! Able to move many tons of gravel very quickly and precisely this addition to our fleet saves the day!
