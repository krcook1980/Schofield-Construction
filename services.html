Rock Hammering

Our fleet of machines include two 5,000 lb hammer/breaker attachments for our 50,000 lb track excavators. These machines have hammered out thousands of yards of solid rock all over the Summit County area. Most excavators don't bother with these high maintenance attachments and out sorce these costs to others like us. We can bid your projects with rock hammering included, hourly, or by the unit price. Please contact us for prices.

Heavy Hauling and Transport

We can haul rock, dirt, gravel, grub, demolition debris, snow, or anything that will fit in our dump truck or the many other end dumps and side dumps that we work with. We have accounts with all of the local rock quarries, gravel pits, and dump sites. We can bid any hauling job by the hour or unit price. Please contact us to help with your hauling needs.

Structural & Horizontal Work

We hold a current E100 engineering license and a B100 building license in the State of Utah which includes all horizontal work including footings, foundations, flat work, etc. to get your project "out of the hole." With our custom home building experience we can even help manage your job through all of the ground stages and get you ready for vertical construction.
